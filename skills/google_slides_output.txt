Google Slides
Design & Layout

Use consistent font families throughout (max 2 fonts: one for headers, one for body)
Apply company/brand colors consistently across all slides
Bold slide titles and make them size 32-40pt
Keep body text at 18-24pt for readability
Use dark text on light backgrounds or white text on dark backgrounds for contrast
Apply a professional theme (Slide > Change theme)

Visual Hierarchy

Place key information in the top-left (where eyes naturally start)
Use color-coded sections: Blue for information, Green for success/positives, Red for warnings/negatives, Yellow for highlights
Add icons or images to break up text-heavy slides
Use bulleted lists with 5-7 words per bullet maximum
Highlight important numbers in bold and larger font size

Content Structure

Limit text to 6 lines per slide (fewer is better)
Use one main idea per slide
Add slide numbers for navigation (Insert > Slide numbers)
Create a table of contents slide for longer presentations
Use transition effects sparingly (Slide > Transition > choose subtle effects)
Add speaker notes for your talking points (View > Show speaker notes)

Professional Touches

Align all elements properly (Arrange > Align)
Maintain consistent spacing between elements
Use master slides for consistent formatting (Slide > Edit theme)
Add your logo on every slide (typically top-right corner)
